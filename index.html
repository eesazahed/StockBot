StockBot

StockBot is a Slack bot that allows you to fetch the most recent price of a stock or cryptocurrency.

  • /stock SYMBOL: fetches the price of a stock based on the symbol
  • /stock SYMBOL CURRENCY: fetches the price of a stock and converts the currency from USD to the chosen currency (example: CAD)
  • /crypto SYMBOL: fetches the price of a cryptocurrency based on the symbol
  • /crypto SYMBOL CURRENCY: fetches the price of a cryptocurrency and converts the currency from USD to the chosen currency (example: CAD)

Hosted on Nest
